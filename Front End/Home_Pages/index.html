    Meenakshi Boys Hostel

    Located near KL University, Meenakshi Boys Hostel has been a trusted accommodation provider for over 5 years. With a capacity of hosting 250+ students, we offer a safe, comfortable, and student-friendly environment at a reasonable fee structure.

    • Conveniently located within walking distance from KL University.
    • Delicious and hygienic meals provided daily.
    • Free essential services like Wi-Fi, laundry, and housekeeping to support students' needs.
    • 24/7 security and a caring staff for a safe and comfortable stay.

    At Meenakshi Boys Hostel, we understand the needs of students and strive to provide a homely and productive environment. Join us to experience a perfect blend of comfort, care, and convenience.
